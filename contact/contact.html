Hi, I’m Amanda! I was born and raised right outside of Baton Rouge, and I love everything southern. My dreams to raise a family, perfect my hair styling craft, and have a good time are coming true!


Hair is hands down my favorite hobby and luckily I get to do it for a living. In 2010 I decided to add extensions to my list of services. I officially became 'Great Lengths Fusion' certified in Miami and 2 years later became 'Great Lengths Cold Fusion' certified in Atlanta.


In those years I understood how extensions can help as well as hurt someone’s hair. Craving more knowledge and seeing how weft extensions were becoming the markets new trend, I looked into yet another extension certification for beaded wefts in 2018. However, 3 certifications later, I was not convinced that these weft extension were as great as promised.


Finally, I found what I was looking for, what all the other methods were lacking. I found Invisible Bead Extensions, a.k.a "I.B.E". In March of 2020 I became certified and the rest was “history”. I.B.E exceeded my, and my clients, expectations. Of course, if you know me by now, I enrolled in their Masters Program to become an elite stylist.


Extensions and color are my true passions in hair styling. Through I.B.E. I’ve been able to create the best hair that I’ve done in my career. After 11 years, I still love getting to see the women who sit in my chair leave with a whole new level of confidence to go on about their day.
