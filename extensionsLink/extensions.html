Invisible Bead Extensions

I.B.E. is the safest method for weft extensions on the market. Style your hair anyway you prefer, including having a hidden ponytail. The key to this method is having both a tension and irritation free application. I.B.E. consists of applying beads to your section then applying hand tied wefts above and below to create a completely seamless application. You can add volume, length, or both to your hair and almost everyone is a candidate. I.B.E. does require maintenance of 7-12 weeks depending on the person. We do not "tighten" or "move up" the wefts as most methods do, but rather reinstall them fresh. I suggest clients repurchasing individual wefts after a few months/reinstalls, this will help from needing to buy a entire new set of hair.
