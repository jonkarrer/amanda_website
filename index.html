Extension

Specialist

I chose to become I.B.E certified because this method solves 3 major problems encountered with the different types of hand-tied extensions.


1st and foremost is the damage to the client's hair and scalp from tension, over-direction and haphazard installs.


2nd is client discomfort due to the contact of beads on the scalp and the common "clamp and crank" beading approach.


3rd is the complete lack of versatility and styling options that clients wearing beaded rows experience. Exposed beads, bonds, and braids are all a woman will see when she with her hair up.


I am the only certified I.B.E professional in the 225 area. Free Consultations!

Color

I believe great hair is not achieved unless your hair color is perfect, and the perfect hair color for you is what compliments your skin tone and your eyes.

​

My approach to color is to minimize damage and stress while creating your new look. Using professional quality, high gloss hair color brands along with protective additives is essential to my process. I am able to maintain the integrity of the hair and promote healthy hair while getting the color you desire.

​

I have mastered my craft through intense education programs for years. To execute methods such as Highlights, Lowlights, Balayage, Ombré, Tint, Color change, and Toner takes practice and patience. I begin every visit with consultation. Communication is the most important and the key ingredient to your perfect visit.
