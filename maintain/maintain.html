Do`s


  1. Sleep in a low pony or braid.
  2. Brush gently every day.
  3. Use a scrunchy or invisibobble when poosible for ponytails.
  4. Use a silk pillow case or head scarf to sleep (will maintain the hair longer.
  5. When brushing, use your free hand to secure the base of the weft.
  6. Deep condition weekly. Bi-weekly if possible.
  7. Check out this link to see more https://youtu.be/XiLRM9_jt04

Do Not


  1. Be overly rough on your extensions while brushing or any other activity.
  2. Avoid chlorinated/salt water. If swimming, dampen hair with TAP water and add a leave in conditioner. Brush through, braid tightly, wash/dry hair after your done swimming.
  3. Avoid sunscreens, tanning oils/lotions on extensions (esp. lighter hair). The hair can turn a pink/coral color and cannot be guaranteed to be removed.
  4. Avoid over use of purple/blue shampoos on the extensions. They will cause them to dry out, and purple/blue color will stain the extensions over time if not immediately.
  5. Avoid sleeping with wet hair.
  6. Avoid fast drying sprays and dry shampoo on extensions.
  7. Avoid protein fill products on the extensions.
  8. Avoid spraying hair spray or dry texture spray on the extensions before heat styling.
  9. NEVER HAVE BLEACH APPLIED TO YOUR EXTENSIONS.

Washing


  1. Brush through the ends before shampooing.
  2. Avoid chlorinated/salt water. If swimming, dampen hair with TAP water and add a leave in conditioner. Brush through, braid tightly, wash/dry hair after your done swimming.
  3. Use salon grade sulfate free shampoo/conditioner.
  4. Do not scrub w/ nails on weft rows, use the pads of finger tips. The scissor method is best. Check this link https://youtu.be/nTJhz0t6n5k to see more tips on washing.
  5. Shampooing in the shower is best/easiest. RINSE, RINSE, RINSE. Make sure all shampoo is removed otherwise irritation at rows can occur.
  6. No conditioner at top of the weft (can cause slipping). Shampooing your actually extension hair less often and only conditioning them will help keep the hair in better condition longer.
  7. Deep condition ends once a week (comb through).


Styling


  1. Use wet/dry oil daily (clear only).
  2. Use a wet brush or boar bristle brush to brush through ends/hair. Make sure when hair is dry you gently brush between rows to help with matting.
  3. Rough dry 80% before starting to brush (focus on tops of wefts).
  4. Apply a leave in conditioner and heat protectant before starting to blow dry.
